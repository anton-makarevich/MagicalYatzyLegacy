Dice Poker - exciting dice game based on the worldwide popular Kniffel (Yatzy) game . The goal is to get as many points as posible by collecting different poker combinations of five dices.
Dice Poker - this is a great opportunity to test your luck and have fun.
Four sets of rules are implemented in the Dice Poker game, which makes it accessible for players with different levels, including children.
